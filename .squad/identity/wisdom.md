# Team Wisdom

Reusable patterns and heuristics learned through work. NOT transcripts — each entry is a distilled, actionable insight.

## Patterns

<!-- Append entries below. Format: **Pattern:** description. **Context:** when it applies. -->

**Pattern:** Always use `ask_user` when handing control back to the CTO or user. Present 2–4 concrete next-step suggestions. Never end a work batch with a wall of text — end it with a structured choice. **Context:** every coordinator handoff, after every ceremony, after every QA sign-off, after every review pipeline run.

**Pattern:** Never ask more than one question at a time via `ask_user`. If multiple decisions are needed, ask the most important one first and chain the rest after the answer. **Context:** whenever gathering requirements, resolving ambiguity, or presenting implementation choices.
